About Us

OUR MISSION
At Blue Haven, we offer a family-like environment with higher standards of care for seniors that cannot live at their own home any longer.
OUR VISION
Our vision is to be the leading provider of senior living options in the communities we serve, offering every individual with a safe and comfortable environment that feels like home.
OUR SERVICES
At ZoeOfJoy, we make sure to provide our residents with a home-like environment to help them transition smoothly. We provide 24/7 care services to help them feel at ease, safe, and healthy in the comfort of our homes. Our team also makes sure to care for them compassionately, helping them enjoy their golden years and independence in the best way possible.
