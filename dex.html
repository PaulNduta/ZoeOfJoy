Meals

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are included every day. Accommodations can be made for any dietary restrictions or special doctor-prescribed diets. Additional food items such as coffee, tea and snacks between meals are also included.

Short-Term / Respite Care:

Zoe of Joy Adult Family Home LLC provides Short-Term/Respite Care services for individuals requiring temporary stays of less than a month. This service is designed to offer relief to caregivers, allowing them to take essential breaks.

Specialized Care for Adults with Developmental Disabilities:

Onsite Rehabilitative Services:

This facility offers in-house rehabilitative services. These services may vary from providing assistance with stretching, range-of-motion, or following a prescribed at-home plan, to a full on-site exercise room with professional equipment and physical therapists. If you need onsite rehabilitative services, please make sure to verify what type of rehab services are offered at this location. Additionally, visiting therapists for scheduled physical therapy treatments may be accommodated.

Housekeeping and Laundry

Zoe of Joy Adult Family Home LLC does all daily housekeeping throughout the home as well as in your room. Laundry is provided for your flat linens on a set schedule or sooner if needed. Your personal laundry is also washed and folded regularly by care staff.
